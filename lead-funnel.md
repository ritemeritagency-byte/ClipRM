# Appointment Setter Form

Use this form to collect appointment requests for applicants who want to apply abroad in:
- UAE, Dubai
- Qatar

Target roles:
- Household Worker
- Babysitter
- House Cook
- Lady Driver

## Form Purpose

This form is for booking appointments and helping applicants connect with Rite Merit.

It can be used for:
- Receive a Call from a Rite Merit Representative
- Visit the Office
- Schedule a Follow-Up Call
- Learning more about available job opportunities

## Required Fields

- Full Name
- Phone Number
- Location
- Desired Position
- Desired Country
- Passport Status
- Appointment Type
- Date
- Time
- Notes

## Simple Lead Flow

```text
Appointment request received
  -> Collect form details
  -> Confirm appointment time
  -> Qualify the applicant
  -> Follow up until booked
```
