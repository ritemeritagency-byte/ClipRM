# Lead Callback Form

Use this form to collect leads who want to apply abroad in:
- UAE, Dubai
- Qatar

Target roles:
- Household Worker
- Babysitter
- House Cook
- Lady Driver

## Form Purpose

This form is for calling leads back or scheduling a visit to the office.

## Required Fields

- Full Name
- Phone Number
- Location
- Desired Position
- Desired Country
- Passport Status
- Schedule for Call or Visit to Office

## Simple Lead Flow

```text
Lead received
  -> Collect form details
  -> Call back or schedule office visit
  -> Qualify the lead
  -> Follow up until placed
```
